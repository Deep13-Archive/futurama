From the Artist

November 4th, 2008

Futurama Vol. 6 - The Movies by Gedeon Maheux

If any television show was canceled before its time, it had to be Fox’s Futurama. The series was the brain child of the Simpson’s creator Matt Groening, was set in the distant future and was to be smarter and geekier than its predecessor from Springfield. Fox never quite “got” the series however and consistently pre-empted the show with Football, moved its time slot all over the place and never really promoted it properly. As a result, many fans like myself missed quite a few episodes and when the entire series was released on DVD, I jumped at the chance to own the entire run.

Futurama Vol. 6 is a mish-mash really, of characters culled from the two direct to DVD movies that have been released to date – Bender’s Big Score and The Beast With A Billion Backs. The set includes Lars Fillmore, Bubblegum Tate, the Globetrotter with a knack for advanced temporal physics, Fry as Yivo and retro versions of Bender and Fry from the “Steamboat Willie” opening of Beast. I hope you enjoy this latest collection, I’m sure they’re will be more to come!
